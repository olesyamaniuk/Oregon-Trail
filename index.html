Step into the Wild West and build your own frontier town in The Oregon Trail: Boom Town! As a pioneer, you’ll manage resources, trade supplies, and help settlers thrive on their journey westward. Grow your settlement, develop businesses, and experience the challenges and excitement of the legendary Oregon Trail. Can you turn a humble village into a booming frontier town?

FAQ

  • Is The Oregon Trail: Boom Town free to play?

    Yes, the game is free with optional in-app purchases for premium items

  • Can I play offline?

    Some features are available offline, but an internet connection is needed for trading and events

  • What makes this game different from other Oregon Trail games?

    This version focuses on town-building and trade while maintaining the spirit of the original journey

  • Is there multiplayer?

    Yes, you can interact with other players, trade, and participate in community events

  • Who developed the game?

    The Oregon Trail: Boom Town is developed by Winner Trail Studios, known for immersive simulation and strategy games
